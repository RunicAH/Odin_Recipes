Ingredients

Dough Ingredients

  • Milk - 3/4 cup warm milk
  • Granualted Sugar - 1/4 granualted sugar
  • Quick Rise Yeast - 2 1/4 teaspoons quick rise of active yeast
  • Butter - 1/4 butter, melted (salted)
  • Egg + Egg youl - 1 egg yolk
  • Flour - 3 cups (plus dusting)
  • Salt - 3/4 teaspoon salt

For the Filling

  • Brown Sugar- 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • Cinnamon - 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • Butter - 1/4 cup butter

For the Cream Cheese Frosting

  • Cream Cheeze - 4oz cream cheese softened
  • Butter - 3 tablespoons butter
  • Powedered Sugar - 3/4 cup
  • Vanilla Extract - 1/2 teaspoon

Note*

After the Dough is complete let it rest for about an 1 hr. Then flattened it out and add the filling generously, make sure you don't go to much on the edges! Next, roll it up and cut it up. Place in a tray and set in the oven for 350 degrees for 20-25 mins.
